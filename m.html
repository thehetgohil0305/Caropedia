McLaren

  • McLaren is a British luxury automaker known for its high-performance sports cars, founded in 1963 by Bruce McLaren. The company is headquartered in Woking, Surrey, and has earned a reputation for producing some of the fastest and most innovative cars in the world.
    Bruce McLaren (Founder)
  • As of 2021, the CEO of McLaren Automotive is Zak Brown, leading the company towards further technological advancements in performance and electric vehicles.
    Zak Brown (CEO)
  • McLaren is heavily involved in motorsports, particularly in Formula 1. The McLaren F1 team has won multiple championships and continues to be a major player in F1 racing.
  • The brand's road cars, such as the McLaren P1, McLaren 720S, and McLaren Artura, are celebrated for their incredible speed, lightweight design, and cutting-edge technology.
