Tata Motors

  • Tata Motors is an Indian multinational automotive manufacturer, founded in 1945. It is a subsidiary of the Tata Group and is headquartered in Mumbai, India. The company produces a wide range of vehicles, including cars, trucks, buses, and electric vehicles.
    Ratan Tata (Former Chairman)
  • As of 2024, the CEO of Tata Motors is Shailesh Chandra, leading the company towards sustainability and electric mobility advancements.
    Shailesh Chandra (CEO)
  • Tata Motors has a strong presence in commercial and passenger vehicles. It owns the luxury brand Jaguar Land Rover and has been a pioneer in the electric vehicle market with models like the Tata Nexon EV.
  • Tata Motors has a long-standing history in the Indian automotive industry and has expanded globally with operations in multiple countries. The brand is known for reliability, innovation, and a commitment to green mobility.
  • The company’s latest models focus on safety, performance, and affordability. Iconic models include the Tata Safari, Tata Harrier, and Tata Nexon , reflecting the company’s advancements in technology and design.
