  • The company has been noted for its continued participation in racing, especially in Formula 1, where its team, Scuderia Ferrari, is the series' single oldest and most successful. Scuderia Ferrari has raced since 1929, first in Grand Prix events and later in Formula 1, where it holds many records. Historically, Ferrari was also highly active in sports car racing, where its cars took many wins in races such as the Daytona, Sebring and 24 Hours of Le Mans, as well as several overall victories in the World Sportscar Championship.
